About James

James is the founder of Kornbluh Consulting.

I serve visionary leaders and teams in business and social enterprise.

I feel like I’ve seen it all, but every day I see new challenges and opportunities through the eyes and experiences of my clients and confidantes.

My business is the business of people in pursuit of shared vision and objectives. This means I work with top leaders to clarify vision, negotiate relationships, and build high performing teams and cultures that execute brilliantly.

Too often my clients spend excessive time, energy and money dealing with difficult partnerships, underperforming leadership teams and distracted, conflicted organizational cultures.

The ultimate objective of my work is to liberate leaders to lead. For that to happen you need the time and space to focus on the big picture. And you need a great leadership team. And you need a culture that provides the foundation for true innovation and exponential growth.
